Supportability:
	Supportability refers to the software's ability to be easily modified or maintained to 
	accommodate typical usage or change scenarios. 
	E.G:
	The system shall allow users to create new workflows without the need for additional programming.

Testability:
	Testability refers to extent of ease with which a system can be tested.
	Higher the testability of system the easier will be finding faults in system.

Serviceability:
	Ability of a system to provide good service to its users.
	Or
	Speed with which a system can be repaired or maintained.
	Software upgrade, hardware upgrade and Documentation are some of the faetures that
	facilitate serviceability.
